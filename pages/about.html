Philosophy

Design Principles

The core beliefs that guide every system I architect

01

Simplicity Over Cleverness

The best systems are boring systems. Complexity is the enemy of reliability. I design for maintainability by the median engineer, not the exceptional one.

02

Security as Enabler

Security should accelerate delivery, not block it. Shift-left security, automated compliance, and golden paths that are secure by default.

03

Platforms Scale People

Technology exists to amplify human capability. Great platforms reduce cognitive load and let engineers focus on business value, not infrastructure.

04

Evidence Over Opinion

Every architecture decision should be backed by data. Observability, metrics, and feedback loops drive continuous improvement.
